## How It Works

### Section heading
From role brief to shortlist in just a few steps

### Section subtitle
HireFlow AI helps recruiting teams move faster by simplifying the hiring workflow from creation to candidate review.

### Steps

1. Create the role
   - Description: Start with a short brief and let AI turn it into a polished job description.

2. Review candidates
   - Description: Upload resumes or import applicants and let AI surface the strongest matches.

3. Shortlist and contact
   - Description: Generate scorecards and outreach drafts so your team can act quickly on top candidates.
