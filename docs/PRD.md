## 1. Product Summary

HireFlow AI is an AI-powered recruiting assistant that helps growing teams create job posts, screen candidates faster, and move qualified applicants through the hiring pipeline with less manual effort. The MVP focuses on reducing the time spent on repetitive recruiting tasks so teams can hire faster and with better consistency.

---

## 2. Target Users

### Primary users
- Recruiters at small to mid-sized companies
- Hiring managers who need faster shortlisting
- Founders or operations leaders handling early-stage hiring

### Secondary users
- Talent acquisition teams with limited bandwidth
- Agencies or contractors managing multiple openings

### User pain points
- Too much time spent writing job descriptions and reviewing resumes
- Inconsistent candidate evaluation
- Slow response times during hiring cycles
- Difficulty prioritizing strong candidates

---

## 3. MVP Features

The MVP should focus on a small set of high-impact workflows:

- AI-generated job descriptions
  - Create polished job posts from a short prompt or company info
- Resume screening assistant
  - Highlight matching candidates based on role requirements
- Candidate scorecard
  - Summarize candidate fit with structured notes and skill match
- AI outreach draft
  - Generate personalized outreach messages for shortlisted candidates
- Simple pipeline view
  - Track candidates by stage: Applied, Screening, Interview, Hired

### MVP scope
- Web app experience
- Basic account creation and team setup
- Upload resumes or import candidate data
- Simple dashboard for active hiring pipelines

### Out of scope for MVP
- Full ATS replacement
- Advanced interview scheduling
- Deep HRIS integrations
- Multi-language support

---

## 4. Value Proposition

HireFlow AI helps hiring teams move from job opening to shortlist in less time by automating the repetitive parts of recruiting. Instead of spending hours on writing, screening, and messaging, teams can focus on the candidates who are most likely to succeed.

**Core promise:** Faster hiring decisions with less manual effort.

## 6. User Flow

### New user flow
1. Visit landing page and sign up
2. Create a company workspace
3. Create a new job opening
4. Enter role details or generate a job description with AI
5. Upload resumes or import candidates
6. Review AI-generated fit scores and summaries
7. Shortlist candidates and generate outreach messages
8. Move candidates through the pipeline

### Ideal user experience
- Fast, guided setup
- Minimal friction
- Clear candidate prioritization
- Simple actions with low cognitive load

- Time to create first job post
- Time to shortlist first candidate
- Percentage of users who complete a full screening workflow
- Repeat usage within 30 days
